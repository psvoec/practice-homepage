The design challenge

The design challenge involved rethinking the existing facade, improving the convoluted interior layout, and enriching each space wiht finely crafted materials. We were required to reuse the existing footprint, while raising the top of foundation walls to exceed new flood regulations. Innovative materials were used to produce an energy efficient thermal envelope andcontemporary yet contextually appropriate facades.
